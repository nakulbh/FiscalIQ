# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Small business owners, freelancers, and individuals who dread tax season. They want to file accurately without hiring a CPA or wrestling with clunky tax software. They value clarity over jargon, speed over feature bloat.

## Product Purpose

Clarifile is an AI-powered tax filing and optimization platform. It reads financial documents, identifies deductions, generates compliant filings, and explains every line in plain language. Success = a filed return the user understands and trusts, completed in minutes instead of hours.

## Brand Personality

Precise, calm, trustworthy. The voice of a competent advisor who never condescends. No urgency tricks, no pop-up pressure. Quiet confidence that the numbers are right.

## Anti-references

- TurboTax's upsell maze and progress-bar anxiety
- H&R Block's cluttered dashboard with competing CTAs
- Generic "fintech dark mode" with neon accents and hype language
- Anything that uses fear of the IRS as a motivator

## Design Principles

1. **Calm authority.** The interface should feel like a well-organized desk, not a countdown timer. Generous whitespace, restrained color, predictable layout.
2. **Show the work.** Every AI-generated suggestion is traceable. Cite sources, show calculations, link back to the original document. Trust comes from transparency.
3. **One thing at a time.** No multi-column dashboards on the landing page. Each section communicates one idea, then yields to the next. Scroll is the narrative.
4. **Earn the click.** CTAs come after the argument, not before. The visitor should understand what Clarifile does and why it's different before being asked to sign up.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA minimum. High contrast on all text. Clear focus indicators. No information conveyed by color alone. Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`.
